Amazing Brownie Recipe

This is a guide to make an amazing gooey brownie.
Make sure to follow this step by step guide on how
to make this delicious brownie.

Shopping List

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt

Cooking

This will take approximately 25 mins to cook.
Additionally will need extra 20 mins to prep

  1. Step 1:
    • Pre heat the oven to 175 on fan
    • Heat up some butter until melted
  2. Step 2:
    • Into a bowl add:
      • 1 Cup sugar
      • 2 Whole eggs
      • 1 Tea spoon of vanilla extract
  3. Step 3:
    • Into another bowl add:
      • Half cup flour
      • 1/3 cup of Cocoa
      • Tea spoon of baking powder
      • Half tea spoon of salt
  4. Step 4:
    • Now combine both bowls made in step 2&3
    • Along with the melted butter and mix!
  5. Step 5:
    • Now place the mixture into a baking tray
    • Cook for 25 mins until soft and gooey inside
  6. Step 6:
    • BONE APPLE TEETH
